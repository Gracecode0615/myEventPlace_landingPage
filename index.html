Frequently Asked Questions and Answers

What is myEventplace?

myEventplace is an all-in-one platform that helps you create, promote, and manage events effortlessly, whether online or in person.

How do I use myEventplace?

Simply sign up, set up your event page, share your link, and track your attendees all from one dashboard.

How do I host an event on myEventplace?

Once you create an account, click “Create Event”, input your event information (title, date, location), choose ticket options, and hit publish. Your event page is now live and ready for guests!

Can I sell tickets through myEventplace?

Definitely! Set the ticket price, choose the number of available slots, and share your event link. Payments are handled securely, so you can focus on creating a great event experience.
